Formula & criteria

Reynolds number:

Re = (ρ · V · D) / μ

Flow regime (for pipe flow):

  • Re < 2300 → laminar
  • 2300 ≤ Re ≤ 4000 → transitional
  • Re > 4000 → turbulent
